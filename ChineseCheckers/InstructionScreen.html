Instructions

Classic

The aim is to race all of one's pieces to the opposite side of the board before your opponents do the same. Each player has 10 pieces. Players take turns moving a single piece either by moving one step in any direction to an adjacent empty space or by jumping in one or any number of available consecutive hops over other single pieces. A player may not combine hopping with a single-step move, for a move consists of one or the other. Turns proceed counterclockwise.
